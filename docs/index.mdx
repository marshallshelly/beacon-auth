## Why BeaconAuth?

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Plugin Architecture" icon="puzzle">
    Extend functionality effortlessly. Only pay for what you use. Includes
    **OAuth**, **2FA**, and **Magic Links** out of the box.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Database Agnostic" icon="seti:db">
    Built-in support for **PostgreSQL**, **MySQL**, **SQLite**, and **MongoDB**.
    Easily adaptable to custom data stores.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Type-Safe Config" icon="setting">
    Leverage Go's strong typing for mistake-free configuration. Intuitive
    `WithOption` pattern.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Framework Ready" icon="rocket">
    Integrates with **Fiber**, **Gin**, **Echo**, **Chi**, and standard
    `net/http`.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<div style="margin-top: 4rem; text-align: center;">

```go
auth, _ := beaconauth.New(
    beaconauth.WithAdapter(postgres.New(dsn)),
    beaconauth.WithPlugins(
        emailpassword.New(),
        twofa.New(),
    ),
)
```
